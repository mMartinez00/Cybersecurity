Why Cybersecurity Matters

This website highlights real-world cyber threats such as phishing, ransomware, account takeovers, and social engineering, and shows why cybersecurity is essential in reducing these risks. By understanding how attacks happen and learning simple protection methods like strong passwords, multi-factor authentication, regular updates, and safe online habits, users can significantly improve their digital safety.

CyberShield is not a security tool, but an educational platform that demonstrates how awareness and basic cybersecurity practices can prevent many common cyber incidents before they cause harm.
